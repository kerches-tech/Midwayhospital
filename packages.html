BACK-TO-SCHOOL- FIRST TIME

Healthy childhood is a foundation for a healthy adulthood. This package is specially designed to detect specific health conditions through a series of relevant physical and laboratory examinations for the growing child.

BASIC: General Practitioner Consultation, Full Blood Count, Blood group, Hb Electrophoresis, RDT for Malaria, Typhoid, Urine Routine Examination, and Stool Routine Examination.

COMPREHENSIVE: BASIC PACKAGE + Dental Consult and Eye Consult + test.

BACK-TO-SCHOOL- ROUTINE

Healthy childhood is a foundation for a healthy adulthood. This package is specially designed to detect specific health conditions through a series of relevant physical and laboratory examinations for the growing child.

BASIC: General Practitioner Consultation, Full Blood Count, RDT for Malaria, Typhoid, Urine Routine Examination, and Stool Routine Examination.

COMPREHENSIVE: • BASIC PACKAGE + Dental Consult and Eye Consult + test.

PRE-EMPLOYMENT PACKAGE

The employee’s health status has a huge impact on their job performance and productivity. This package allows for a basic understanding of the prospective employee’s suitability for the role and also helps optimize their health.

AREAS WE COVER: We cover areas such as Full Blood Count, Sickling Test, Typhoid, Urine Routine Examination, Eye test, Hepatitis B, HIV, Hepatitis A, and Stool Routine Examination

WHO IS THIS FOR: Administrative Role, Nanny/Househelp, Cook/Kitchen Staff

BASIC HEALTH CHECK UP

Minimum array of tests recommended for individuals of any age group for a basic health assessment.

AREAS WE COVER: General Practitioner consultation & Physical Examination, Full Blood Count, Fasting blood sugar, Blood group, Hepatitis B Surface Antigen Test, Typhoid Antibody Test, RDT for Malaria, and Urine RE.

STI SCREEN

Many sexually transmitted infections (STIs) do not cause any symptoms. Left untreated, STIs may cause long-term complications. Protect your health and the health of your partner by getting tested.

MALE: HIV, Hepatitis B, VDRL, Chlamydia, Gonorrhoea, Urine R/E.

FEMALE: HIV, Hepatitis B, VDRL, Chlamydia, Gonorrhoea, Urine R/E, HVS R/E.

DIABETES/HYPERTENSION CHECK UP

Poorly controlled Diabetes and Hypertension can cause serious long-term complications such as strokes, heart attacks, kidney failure, blindness etc. This package is designed for early detection of complications of patients living with diabetes and/or hypertension.

BASIC: General Practitioner Consultation & Physical Examination, Full Blood Count, Fasting Blood Sugar, Kidney Function test, Lipid Profile-100, Liver Function Test, Liver Function Test, ECG, and Urine Routine Examination

COMPREHENSIVE: Physician Specialist Consultation & Physical Examination, Full Blood Count, Fasting Blood Sugar, HbA1C, Kidney Function test, Lipid Profile, Liver Function Test, ECG, Urine Routine Examination, Dietician Consultation, and Eye Consultation.

PRE-MARITAL HEALH CHECK UP

Premarital screening ensures the couple are healthy, helps detect and limit the spread of certain genetic blood conditions (e.g. Sickle Cell Disease), Rhesus Incompatibility and also detects and treats chronic infectious diseases and reduces the risk of transmission to partner.

MALE: General Practitioner consultation, Full Blood Count, Fasting Blood Sugar, Blood group, Hb Electrophoresis, Hepatitis B Surface Antigen test, HIVm and VDRL.

FEMALE: Fasting Blood Sugar, Hb Electrophoresis, Blood group, Hepatitis B Surface Antigen test, HIV, VDRL, High Vaginal Swab RE, and Pelvic Ultrasound scan.

BASIC PRECONCEPTION PACKAGE

This package assesses the couple for fertility, optimises the health of the couple before embarking on the pregnancy journey. It also helps detect and limit the spread of certain genetic blood conditions (e.g. Sickle Cell Disease), Rhesus Incompatibility and also detects and treats chronic infectious diseases and reduces the risk of transmission to partner

MALE: Fertility Specialist Consultation, Full Blood Count, Fasting Blood Sugar, and Semen Analysis & C/S.

FEMALE: Fertility Specialist Consultation, Full Blood Count, Fasting Blood Sugar, HIV, VDRL, Urine Routine Exam, Serum Prolactin, LH, FSH, Thyroid Function Test, High Vaginal Swab RE, and Pelvic Ultrasound Scan.

EXECUTIVE HEALTH CHECK UP (40 years +)

Covers Comprehensive medical history and physical examination by a specialist. It also includes specific cancer screening for men and women as well as investigations for the early detection of hypertension, diabetes, kidney disease.

MALE: Physician Specialist consultation & Physical Examination, Full Blood Count, Fasting Blood Sugar, Urine Routine Examination, Lipid Profile, Kidney function test, Liver function test, Prostate Specific Antigen, ECG, Abdominopelvic USG scan, Dental Consultation, and Eye Consultation & Test.

FEMALE: Ob/Gyn Consultation & Physical Examination, Full Blood Count, Fasting blood sugar, Urine Routine Examination, Lipid Profile, Kidney function test, Liver function test, Pap Smear, ECG, Abdominopelvic Ultrasound, Dental Consultation, and Eye Consultation & Test
