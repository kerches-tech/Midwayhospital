Providing Quality Healthcare since 1980

Welcome to Midway Hospital

Midway Hospital was established over four decades ago on the 6th of August 1980 to provide high standard, cost effective and reasonable healthcare services to improve the health status of the community

We started as a 7-bed capacity clinic, originally located at Abeka junction. In October 1988, the Clinic moved to its present location at Achimota and has since been expanding to provide quality healthcare.

We attained hospital status in 2021 and have expanded our services to include multiple specialist outpatient clinics, surgical theatres, labour and delivery suites and a 35-bed capacity ward. With our team of highly trained healthcare professionals dedicated to transforming lives through patient-centered care.
