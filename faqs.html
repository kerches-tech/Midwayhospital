FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions


We understand that you may have questions, and we're here to help! Our Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) page is designed to provide quick, clear answers to some of the most common queries about our services, products, and policies.


Can I just walk in to be attended to?

Yes, the General Medical Practice runs 24-hr services and various specialists have their consulting hours as shown.

Do I need to book an appointment?

For specialist consultation, booking an appointment helps to reduce your waiting time. Alternatively, you can walk-in during their stated consultation hours.

How do I book an appointment?

Call 0548348900 or Click here to Book Appointment Online

What types of specialists and services do you have available?

Please check our specialist schedule page.

How do I locate the Hospital?

We are located on Tetteh-Ashong Street in Abofu-Achimota,150m before the New Achimota Transport terminal. Click here to view our location on Google Maps

How do I book an ambulance?

Call 0548348900 or 0302-950173

Where do I go when I arrive?

Report to the reception/front desk on arrival for onward assistance.

What payment modalities do you accept?

We accept cash payments as well as digital payments; mobile money.

Please check our insureance partners
